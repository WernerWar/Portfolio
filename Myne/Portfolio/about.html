About Me:

Since they day I could remember, I was playing video games. I probably played them a little too much, but I loved everything about them, completing puzzles, defending worlds, shooting monsters, finding cool items and much more. All of things could be done on a small screen infront of me, some of my best memories are playing video games with my friends. Growing up I never lost my love for video games and decided to build my career around them by becoming a video game designer, by being able to design them myself I can use my creativity and make fun interactive games for people to play, and maybe I'll even inspire the next generatioln of video game designers. My dream is to start my own video game company or get a job opportunity at GGG(Grinding Gear Games), a video game company located in New Zealand that made "Path of Exile", my most favourite game to date. I specialize more in the coding and mechanical side of creating video games, but I would really like to be a skill/ability developer.
